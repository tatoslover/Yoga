{% if target == "physical" %}

Improved Flexibility

Regular practice gradually loosens tight muscles and increases range of motion in joints.

Increased Strength

Many yoga poses require supporting your body weight in new ways, building functional strength.

Better Posture

As your flexibility and strength improve, your posture follows. Many poses develop core strength.

Joint Health

Yoga moves joints through their full range of motion, helping prevent degenerative arthritis.

{% elif target == "mental" %}

Stress Reduction

Yoga is known for its ability to reduce stress and promote relaxation. Practice encourages focusing on the present.

Anxiety Relief

Research suggests yoga can reduce anxiety. The combination of movement, breathwork, and meditation creates a powerful tool.

Improved Focus

Regular yoga practice improves coordination, reaction time, memory, and even IQ scores.

Mood Enhancement

Studies have found that yoga can reduce depression and increase serotonin levels.

{% elif target == "lifestyle" %}

Healthier Habits

Yoga often leads practitioners to adopt healthier lifestyles naturally, including better eating habits.

Weight Management

Regular practice can support healthy weight management by reducing stress and increasing mindfulness around food choices.

Improved Breathing

Yoga teaches proper breathing techniques that can be applied throughout daily life, improving lung function.

Community Connection

Participating in group classes provides social benefits and a sense of belonging, important for long-term health.
